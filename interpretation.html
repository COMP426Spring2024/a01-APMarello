Interpretation

While there are many interpretations of the story of Prometheus, the themes of sacrifice, defiance, and the advocation of human progress and knowledge remain consistent. Prometheus was a titan and one who represented semi-divinity and was subservient to the gods of Olympus. His actions of stealing fire represent a rebellion against divine authority. It can be seen as a representation of the values of freedom and the challenges of authoritative hierarchies.
