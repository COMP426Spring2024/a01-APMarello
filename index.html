Welcome to the Prometheus Site

Prometheus is a significant figure in the world of Greek mythology. A titan known for his intelligence, he was tasked by the gods to create humans on Earth. His narrative exhibits acts of honor, defiance, and suffering. Learn about the story of Prometheus, the giver of fire.
